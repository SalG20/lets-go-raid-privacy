Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 23, 2026  ·  Package: com.peacefulsoil.letsgoraid

This Privacy Policy explains how Let's Go Raid ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App. By using Let's Go Raid, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and authentication data — email address, authentication identifiers, session tokens, and related account records managed through Supabase Auth.
  • Profile and trainer data — trainer name, trainer level, trainer code, avatar or style choices, verification status, premium or ad-free subscription status, moderation or suspension status, and related profile settings you provide.
  • Raid and coordination activity — hosted raids, joined raids, party status, selected raid boss, team color, party size, raid timing, ready/join status, and related coordination data created when you use the App.
  • Verification images — screenshots or images you voluntarily submit for profile verification (for example, in-game screenshots used to verify trainer level or other details). These are only processed if you choose to use the verification feature.
  • Device and app diagnostics — app version, device platform and operating system, error logs, crash reports, and debugging information used to operate and improve the App.
  • Purchase and subscription data — Google Play purchase or subscription identifiers, RevenueCat customer identifiers, entitlement status (premium / remove-ads), product identifiers, and related billing events needed to provide paid features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide account access, authentication, and session management.
  • Enable raid hosting, joining, party coordination, and related App features.
  • Verify profiles and improve trust, safety, and community quality.
  • Detect and moderate abuse, spam, scam behavior, and violations of our terms.
  • Process subscriptions and provide premium or ad-free features.
  • Debug technical issues, respond to support requests, and maintain App reliability.
  • Improve the App based on usage patterns and diagnostic data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party service providers to operate. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and terms of service:

  • Supabase — provides authentication, database storage, Edge Functions, and backend services. Data is stored and processed on Supabase infrastructure. (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription entitlements and in-app purchase validation for premium features. (RevenueCat Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing — processes Android in-app purchases and subscriptions. Subject to Google's privacy policies and terms. (Google Privacy Policy)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers listed above that are necessary to operate the App.
  • When required by applicable law, legal process, court order, or platform policy.
  • To investigate abuse, safety incidents, fraud, or security threats.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and profile data are retained while your account remains active.
  • Raid activity and operational logs may be retained as needed for app operation, safety enforcement, debugging, and abuse prevention.
  • Verification images are retained as needed to support the verification process and moderation, and may be deleted upon account deletion request.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. See Section 7 (Contact and Data Requests) below.

6. Children's Privacy

Let's Go Raid is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable law to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at peacefulsoil4k@gmail.com.
