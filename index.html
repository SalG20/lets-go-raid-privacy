Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Let's Go Raid ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App. By using Let's Go Raid, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect
  • Account and authentication data — email address, authentication identifiers, session tokens, and related account records managed through Supabase Auth.
  • Profile and trainer data — trainer name, trainer level, trainer code, avatar or style choices, verification status, premium or ad-free subscription status, moderation or suspension status, and related profile settings you provide.
  • Raid and coordination activity — hosted raids, joined raids, party status, selected raid boss, team color, party size, raid timing, ready/join status, and related coordination data created when you use the App.
  • Verification images — if you use the profile verification feature, you may select a screenshot from your device. Images are processed locally on your device using on-device text recognition (OCR) to extract your trainer name and level. No image is uploaded to or stored on our servers. Only the extracted text is transmitted to verify your profile.
  • Device and app diagnostics — app version, device platform and operating system, error logs, and debugging information used to operate and improve the App.
  • Purchase and subscription data — if paid features are enabled, Google Play purchase or subscription identifiers, RevenueCat customer identifiers, entitlement status, product identifiers, and related billing events needed to provide paid features.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • Provide account access, authentication, and session management.
  • Enable raid hosting, joining, party coordination, and related App features.
  • Verify profiles and improve trust, safety, and community quality.
  • Detect and moderate abuse, spam, misleading raids, and violations of our terms.
  • Check premium entitlements if paid features are enabled in your installed app version.
  • Debug technical issues, respond to support requests, and maintain App reliability.
3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party service providers to operate. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — provides authentication, database storage, Edge Functions, and backend services. (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription entitlements and in-app purchase validation. (RevenueCat Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing — processes Android in-app purchases and subscriptions. (Google Privacy Policy)
4. Advertising

Let's Go Raid does not currently display ads. A future optional rewarded ad feature may be added in a later version. If ads are introduced, this policy will be updated before activation to describe what advertising networks are used and what data they may collect.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers listed above that are necessary to operate the App.
  • When required by applicable law, legal process, court order, or platform policy.
  • To investigate abuse, safety incidents, fraud, or security threats.
6. Data Retention
  • Account and profile data are retained while your account remains active.
  • Raid activity and operational logs may be retained as needed for app operation, safety enforcement, and debugging.
  • Verification images are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers. Extracted verification data (trainer name and trainer level) is stored as part of your profile and deleted when your account is deleted.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. See the Account and Data Deletion section below.
7. Children's Privacy

Let's Go Raid is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us at peacefulsoil4k@gmail.com so we can promptly delete it.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to help protect your information. All API traffic uses HTTPS. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable law to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at peacefulsoil4k@gmail.com.

10. Contact and Data Requests

For privacy questions, data access requests, or deletion requests, contact us at peacefulsoil4k@gmail.com. See the Account and Data Deletion section below for step-by-step instructions.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised policy here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights
Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account or specific data at any time — even if you no longer have the app installed. We target completion within 30 days of a complete and verified request.

In-app deletion (fastest)
In app versions that include this feature: go to Settings → Delete Account, confirm the warning, then type DELETE to permanently remove your account and all associated data.

If you no longer have the app installed, or this feature is not yet in your version, email us:

Step 1
Send an email
Email peacefulsoil4k@gmail.com from the address linked to your account.
Step 2
Use the right subject line
Account deletion: "Account Deletion Request – Let's Go Raid"

Data only: "Data Deletion Request – Let's Go Raid"
Step 3
Include your details
Your account email and trainer name (if applicable). We may verify account ownership before processing.
What gets deleted
Account deletion removes: Supabase Auth account, trainer profile (name, level, code, avatar, verification status), profile verification data, and raid history associated with your account. Verification screenshots are processed on your device only and are not uploaded to our servers.

Data-only deletion removes specific data you identify — such as verification images, profile fields, or support records — without deleting your account.

Purchase and payment records managed by Google Play or RevenueCat are subject to their respective policies and may not be removable on request.

5. Raid Hosting and Joining Rules
  • Hosts should create raid listings only for real raids they intend to coordinate.
  • Hosts should keep party settings accurate and avoid abusing cancel, start, party size, minimum level, premium-only, or report flows.
  • Guests should join only raids they intend to participate in and should mark Ready only when actually ready.
  • If your plans change, leave the raid room so the host has an accurate party list.
6. User Conduct
  • Do not harass, threaten, impersonate, spam, exploit, automate abuse, or misuse trainer codes.
  • Do not create fake or misleading raids, submit false reports, or abuse moderation tools.
